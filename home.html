Background

Mount Kibo Schools was established on November 1996 with only 9 students enrolled. In 1997 the first Class One was enrolled with 17 Students. The School has grown since then and transformed as years went by and currently the School has more than 1000 Students registered from Baby Class to Class Seven. The School has further increased its services line and currently serves as a Co-Education Day and Boarding School.

Why Mount Kibo Schools?

We believe Success is not only measured by academic achievement but also by the mark that each student makes on the lives of others. Our staff teaches and inspires excellence in academics and life- long learning by continually reviewing and building an enriched curriculum, promoting student achievement, modeling kindness and integrity while nurturing a desire to learn and practice.
